History Services About Us
Company Name: Sukhoi Company (JSC) Founded: 1939 by Pavel Sukhoi Parent Company: United Aircraft Corporation (UAC) Products: Military aircraft, Civilian aircraft, Unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs)

Key Aircraft

Sukhoi is best known for its highly maneuverable and advanced fighter jets, many of which are used by the Russian military and exported to various countries including India, China, and Algeria.

Military Aircraft


Su-27 (Flanker): A highly successful air superiority fighter that formed the basis for many subsequent variants.


Su-30 (Flanker-C/G/H): A twin-engine, two-seat, supermaneuverable multirole fighter, with specialized variants like the Su-30MKI developed for the Indian Air Force.


Su-34 (Fullback): A twin-engine, two-seat, all-weather supersonic medium-range fighter-bomber/strike aircraft.


Su-35 (Flanker-E/M): A single-seat, twin-engine, 4.5 generation air superiority fighter featuring thrust-vectoring engines.


Su-57 (Felon): Russia's first operational fifth-generation stealth multirole fighter aircraft, designed for air combat and ground strikes.


Su-75 (Checkmate): A prototype of a single-engine stealth fighter currently under development.

Civilian Aircraft


Sukhoi Superjet 100 (SSJ 100): A regional jet airliner, originally designed by Sukhoi Civil Aircraft, which has since been integrated into the Irkut Corporation and rebranded as the SJ-100.
